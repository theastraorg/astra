Learn about us

We are a hackathon ecosystem dedicated to incubating the next generation of leading technical talents, providing both in-person and virtual experiences. In collaboration with our partners, we organize monthly hackathons, each focused on a distinct area of technology.

  • 1. Our Mission

    We aim to equip the next generation of creatives and builders by creating a vibrant community for sharing ideas, imparting practical skills, and exploring the potential of emerging technologies through hackathons.

  • 2. Hackathon Ecosystem

    We host regular global hackathons every month, offering both in-person and virtual experiences, each focusing on a specific area of technology.

  • 3. Our Community

    We're a group of developers, designers, and entrepreneurs working together to shape the future of technology. At Astra, we collaborate and innovate to drive the growth of emerging tech.
