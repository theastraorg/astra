Benefits of Being Part of Astra

Perks so good, you'll never want to leave Astra. Seriously.

  • Networking Opportunity

    Network with professionals, mentors, and peers to exchange ideas, seek advice, and explore collaborations in a supportive environment.

  • Project Showcase

    Project showcasing is an opportunity to share your work with others. It's a chance to display what you've created, get feedback, and be recognized for your efforts.

  • Astra Hackathons

    Astra Hackathons are events where people work together on projects, solve problems, and learn new skills quickly. Winners receive cash prizes and potential investment.

  • Career Boost

    Astra provides a career boost by connecting you with job opportunities. Partnering with CrackedDevs, we help founders assemble their engineering teams.

  • Exclusive Events

    Exclusive events at Astra offer specialized workshops conducted by industry professionals, providing valuable opportunities level up your skills.

  • Fellowship

    We have partnered with Localhost to scout potential fellowship members with strong technical capabilities through Astra Hackathons.
