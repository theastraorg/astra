Privacy Policy

Updated on Tuesday, April 16, 2024

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share information gathered about you in connection with your use of the Astra website and participation in Astra events. By accessing our website or participating in our events, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information Collection

Personal Information: We collect personal information you provide to us when you register for an event, use our website, or communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide.

Non-Personal Information: We may also collect non-personal information, such as browser type, operating system, and website usage statistics, through cookies and other tracking technologies.

2. Use of Information

Your information is used to:

  • Communicate with you about events, services, and updates.
  • Facilitate your participation in Astra events.
  • Improve and personalize your experience on our website.
  • For promotional purposes, in accordance with the Intellectual Property section of our Terms and Conditions.

3. Information Sharing

We may share your information:

  • With event partners and sponsors, for the purpose of facilitating the event.
  • With service providers who perform services on our behalf.
  • When required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Astra Org, our users, or others.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate measures to protect the security of your personal information both online and offline. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on our website. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by us. You can also object to the processing of your personal information, request a limitation on its use, or request its portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the address provided below.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our treatment of your personal information, please contact us here.

We are committed to working with you to obtain a fair resolution of any complaint or concern about privacy.

Acknowledgment

By using our website and participating in Astra events, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
