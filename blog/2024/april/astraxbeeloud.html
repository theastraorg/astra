Announcing the Astra X Beeloud AI Hackathon

Can you build the next billion dollar startup in 3 days?

Last updated on April 26, 2024

Today, we're incredibly excited to announce a major AI Hackathon happening in-person in Vancouver as well as in London and virtually!

Introduction

In a world where you can transition from a prompt to anything in seconds, anything is possible if you can imagine it. Let's show the world what great applications can be built in 3 days aka effective acceleration of AI.

We're bridging major startup hubs like SF, with amazing talent from SF, NYC, Bangalore, London, and other major hubs joining in. You just need to have one person physically on your team from Vancouver. Let's build great things together!

This 3 day hackathon = 6 months of solo work and also World-class companies came out of hackathons.

Goal

From May 10-12, join us as we leap from idea to MVP to user, collaborating to innovate with AI or through AI. Bring your ideas or skills to Beeloud and help shape the next billion-dollar company!

Teams of up to 5 members are welcome, with one member proficient in coding (or utilizing no-code tools) to develop the MVP, and another member focused on user acquisition through building in public, content creation, and crafting a compelling landing page. Join forces to bring your ideas to life and attract users to your project!

Participation

Startup founders with new ideas or ongoing projects, tech developers skilled in turning ideas into reality, no-code/low-coders familiar with Bubble, Flutterflow, or Zapier, creative designers proficient in UI/UX or Canva, data scientists with a grasp on numbers and AI/ML workflows, and content creators adept at building in public or creating viral content are all invited to join us.

Please note that this hackathon is both virtual and in-person. Visitor visa wait times may vary, potentially extending several months based on the country of departure. We recommend applying for your visa immediately upon receiving acceptance to Astra X Beeloud AI Hackathon. For international participants, excluding U.S. citizens, commence your visa application promptly upon receiving an offer.
