Announcing the Winners of Astra X Beeloud AI Hackathon

Astra X Beeloud Global AI Hackathon Winners Announced

Last updated on May 15, 2024

Astra is thrilled to announce the results of the Astra X Beeloud AI Hackathon, a global IRL + Virtual AI Hackathon focused on fostering the next generation of AI startups, projects, and builders. This was Astra's first Global IRL Hackathon, which took place at three global startup hubs: London, SF and Vancouver + Virtual, bringing together founders and developers from around the world to build products.

More than 300 participants teamed up to submit 24 final projects to the judges and investors. This global hackathon featured over $60k in prizes and attracted people from all over the world.

Without further ado, here are the winners…

Main Prize

Congratulations to Team PeakMind for winning the main prize in the "You Are The Loudest" category. Thank you for participating in our Global Hybrid AI Hackathon.

Most Innovative

Congratulations to Mr Sebastien for winning the most innovative prize in the "You Are The Most Innovative" category. Thank you for participating in our Global Hybrid AI Hackathon.

Most Impactful

There are two companies winning in this category. Congratulations to Kindsettler and Carelink for winning the most innovative prize in the "You Are The Most Impactful" category. Thank you for participating in our Global Hybrid AI Hackathon.

Best Use of ICP

Congratulations to Team PeakMind for winning the best use prize in the "Best Use of ICP" category. Thank you for participating in our Global Hybrid AI Hackathon.

Second Best Use of ICP

Congratulations to Team Med AI for winning the best use prize in the "Second Best Use of ICP" category. Thank you for participating in our Global Hybrid AI Hackathon.

Best Use of Couchbase

Congratulations to Team Kind Settle for winning the best use prize in the "Best Use of Couchbase" category. Thank you for participating in our Global Hybrid AI Hackathon.

Best Use of Nexus GPT

Congratulations to Team Med AI for winning the best use prize in the "Best Use of Nexus GPT" category. Thank you for participating in our Global Hybrid AI Hackathon.

Loudest Member

Congratulations to Team Katie Hong for winning the member prize in the "You Are The Loudest Member" category. Thank you for participating in our Global Hybrid AI Hackathon.

Thanks again to all the participants, the judges who reviewed projects, and the sponsors.
But most of all, congratulations to all the winners & participants!
