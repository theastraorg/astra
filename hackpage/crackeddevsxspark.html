CrackedDevs X Spark / Virtual Hackathon / Mar 15 - 17, 2024

CrackedDevs X Spark Hackathon 2024

Introduction

CrackedDevs & Spark are back! - with a 2 day virtual hackathon from the March 15th - 17th, 2024. You can build what you want and win awesome prizes!!
We have some amazing prizes such as:

  1. $1k cash and up to $30k investment for best project!
  2. 20 CodeDamn.com yearly premium memberships (worth $20k)
  3. A guaranteed job interview at a tech company

Build projects, make friends and win prizes!

What is CrackedDevs.com?

CrackedDevs.com is a jobs site to help developers find remote coding jobs! We also have an awesome community where we help you get a coding job. Our community can help you by:

  1. Free Resume/CV reviews
  2. Talks with CEO speakers and recruiters
  3. Mock Interviews

How to participate?

Secure your spot in the hackathon by registering here.

  1. Join CrackedDevs Discord here.
  2. Join Spark Discord here.

Requirements:

For project submission, please ensure adherence to the following criteria:

  1. Github repository link (Public repository).
  2. Demo Video hosted on YouTube or Loom, is required for submission
  3. Optionally, include a link to your deployment.

Prizes:

$40,000 in prizes

Main Prize - No Theme
A cash prize of $1,000 along with a potential further investment of up to $30,000!

CodeDamn Award - Education Track
A free yearly pro subscription to Codedamn.com, worth $500.

Best use of api.crackeddevs.com

Judging Criteria:

  1. Creativity Projects that are new concepts, not currently done before.
  2. Style Projects that are well styled and have nice UI/UX.
  3. Useful Projects that could work in the real world, and help solve a problem.
